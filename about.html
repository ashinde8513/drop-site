    About Drop

    Built for people who go out.

    Right now, finding out what's happening in your city means checking a venue's Instagram, a promoter's TikTok, a resale app, a ticketing site, and a group chat, and still missing the show your friends went to. EDM discovery is scattered across six different apps, none of which know who you actually go out with.

    Drop puts it in one place: real events from real venues and promoters, plus the social layer that tells you who's already going. Every show you go to gets logged automatically, so your nights out add up to an actual history instead of disappearing into a camera roll.

    What we care about

    • Fans first. Drop exists for the people going to shows, not the platforms selling them.
    • Real prices. No hidden fees dropped in at checkout — if a price is shown, it's the price.
    • No spam. Listings are reviewed before they go live, and we don't sell your data or your inbox.

    Where we're based

    Drop is built in Colorado by Resonance Ventures LLC. We're starting in a handful of U.S. cities and expanding from there.
