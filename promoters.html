    Submit your events

    Listing on Drop is free. A real person reviews every submission before it goes live, so you won't end up next to a bot listing or a fake venue. Send us the details and we'll get it posted.

    Include: event name, venue, date and time, lineup, and a link to buy tickets. A flyer or event image helps it stand out, but isn't required.
